Project Overview

This application is to serve as an information hub for those looking to get into the campus gaming club, Niner Esports.

The intended audience are current students of UNCC and incoming freshmen of UNCC, as well as potential sponsors.

Pages Included:

  • Home Page: Central page for navigation.
  • About Page: Description of the organization and what it provides.
  • Contact Page: Form for business related inquiry/sponsorship.
  • Past Events: Detail events from the past 6 months.
  • Lan Events: Overview of the biggest event the club puts on.

    Home Page

  • Purpose: Serve as the central page for the website.

  • Audience: Current students of UNCC and incoming freshmen of UNCC, as well as potential sponsors.

  • Content: Short description of the club. (Will have a navbar header and social media linked footer.)

  • Data to enter: Search bar in top left to find specific content on pages.

  • Validation: String cannot be more than 30 characters, must be a String.

  • Interaction: Users can click on navbar links to direct to other pages on the site as well as to social media. Search based on value.

    About Page

  • Purpose: Provide an overview of the club.

  • Audience: Current students of UNCC and incoming freshmen of UNCC, as well as potential sponsors.

  • Long description of the club. (Will have a navbar header and social media linked footer.)

  • Data to enter: Search bar in top left to find specific content on pages.

  • Validation: String cannot be more than 30 characters, must be a String.

  • Interaction: Users can click on navbar links to direct to other pages on the site as well as to social media. Search based on value.

    Contact Page

  • Purpose: Provide a form to contact the club and to receive club updates and event information.

  • Audience: Current students of UNCC and incoming freshmen of UNCC, as well as potential sponsors.

  • Content: Large background image and short description of the club. (Will have navbar header and social media linked footer)

  • Data to enter: Search bar in top left to find specific content on pages. Phone number, Email address. Form to fill out with personal contact information and a checkbox to subscribe to email newsletter. (Will have navbar header and social media linked footer.)

  • Validation: String cannot be more than 30 characters, must be a String. Email must be valid email. If data is entered for a phone number, it must follow the format of xxx-xxx-xxxx.

  • Users can click on navbar links to direct to other pages on the site as well as to social media. Search based on value. Users can enter email address, phone number (optional.)

    Games We Play Page

  • Purpose: Serve as a gallery of games with their own descriptions.

  • Audience: Current students of UNCC and incoming freshmen of UNCC, as well as potential sponsors.

  • Content: Image gallery with arrow selector that presents the game and its description. Will also include navbar, footer and search bar.

  • Data to enter: Search bar in top left to find specific content on pages.

  • Validation: String cannot be more than 30 characters, must be a String.

  • Users can click on navbar links to direct to other pages on the site as well as to social media. Search based on value.
